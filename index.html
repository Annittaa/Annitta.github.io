    • Zhu ZhengTing

    Zhu ZhengTing(March 18th 1996-?),c-pop star under YueHua Entertainment, debuted on April 6th with NINEPERCENT.

    • Early life

      • Zhu ZhengTing was born in Anhui,China. At the age of 9, Zhu started learning Chinese Classic dance. He also learned ballet at 12, and modern dance at 16. In June 2014, when Zhu was 18, he participated in Chinese Dance Competetion and got the first prize. In September, Zhu was admitted to Shanghai Theater Academy as the Top1 student with the highest test score. Zhu ZhengTing won the first prize in "xingquan bei"student dance contest for his solo dance performace, and became the only freshman who got the prize in this contest.

      Zhu ZhengTing's solo dance
    • Career

      • Zhu became a trainee in YueHua Entertainment since 2015. In 2017,Zhu participated in a Korean survival boy band show,Produce 101, but eventually eliminated on episode 8 and lost the chance to debut. In 2018 Chinese boy band show, Idol Trainees , Zhu shows his great talent in dancing. Because of his efforts and wonderful performances, Zhu garnered a total vote of 11,938,786 in the last episode, and finally debuted as a member of NINEPERCENT.
